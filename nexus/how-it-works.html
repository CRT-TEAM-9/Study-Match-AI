The Matching Equation

We use a strict weighted formula to prevent random or low-value connections. Every single match is given an overall compatibility score.

Compatibility Score Formula Score = (C_s × 0.40) + (O_t × 0.30) + (S_l × 0.15) + (G_y × 0.15)
